Manual publish checklist

1. Deploy the latest dist folder to Vercel.
2. Confirm the live URL is https://cosmittocoffee.vercel.app/.
3. Open https://cosmittocoffee.vercel.app/robots.txt and confirm it loads.
4. Open https://cosmittocoffee.vercel.app/sitemap.xml and confirm it loads.
5. Test the Open Graph preview with:
   https://www.opengraph.xyz/
   https://cards-dev.twitter.com/validator
6. Confirm the favicon appears in the browser tab.
7. Test the site on iPhone Safari and Android Chrome.
8. Test the menu page and every navbar link after deployment.
9. Confirm the footer social links open Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, and Spotify correctly.
10. Confirm the contact email, +216 55 046 609 phone number, address, opening hours, and prices are final.
11. If any hero video fails on mobile, host the MP4 files on Vercel storage, Cloudinary, or another direct-video host.
12. Submit https://cosmittocoffee.vercel.app/sitemap.xml in Google Search Console.
